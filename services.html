Every dog is different. What may work for one dog, may cause bigger problems in another. I believe that all lessons must be uniquely tailored to fit both the temperament and intelligence of the dog, and the wants and needs of its family. Whether your dog is food, play, or attention motivated, we will work together to come up with training plans that give your dog the best chances at success! However, training also requires time and patience from the humans, so it is also important to work closely with you, to ensure you are comfortable with the training and will be able to continue practicing the lessons between sessions.

I only practice force free and positive reinforcement methods in my training. I believe in training dogs by encouraging the behavior we want, not by punishing the behavior we do not. Punishments can lead to a stressed, anxious dog, which often leads to unwanted behaviors getting worse, and can even introduce new problems. In our lessons, I will be guiding you on how to use, and time, a clicker and rewards to guide your dog to the behavior we want to see.

Training

Through my time running and assisting with group classes, as well as one-on-one lessons, I have developed a strong foundation for working with a wide variety of dogs - breeds, personalities and behavioral issues. While not a complete list, below I have included some of my specialties.

  • Basic obedience
  • Leash manners
  • Recall
  • Dancing
  • Resource guarding
  • Leash reactivity

My lessons are usually an hour long, but this may vary depending on the dog. Learning can be exhausting, and your dog may need a break before we reach the end of our sessions. In order to ensure that I can help you and your dog in the most effective way, I offer an initial meet-and-greet session at half price. In this session we will discuss what you hope to get out of the training, and I can spend some time getting to know your pup and evaluating their needs. From this session, I will be able to get a feel for what the training plan will look like for your dog going forward.

  • Initial meet and greet (1 hour): £15
  • One-on-one session (1 hour): £30 (or £130 if you pay for 5 sessions upfront)
  • One-on-one session (1/2 hour): £20
  • Training walks (1 hour): £25
  • Virtual session (1 hour): £25
  • Virtual session (1/2 hour): £20

Service Area

While based in Broadway, I also offer training sessions for dog owners across Worcestershire and Gloucestershire. If you wish to meet in a location more than 5 miles outside of Broadway, there will be a £1 per mile surcharge (one way).
