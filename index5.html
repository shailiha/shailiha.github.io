About

Welcome to Broadway Dog Trainer. I am a certified trainer offering services in and around Broadway, Evesham and Chipping Campden. I offer one-on-one training classes, to help you guide your dog to being a happy, socialized and well mannered pup.

I train a wide range of dogs, from young puppies to adults, people-shy to jump-on-everyone, and nervous walkers to constant pullers and leash reactive pups. I will work closely with both your dog, and you, to ensure that your dog is set up for success from the get-go.

Dog Training

I only use force-free, positive reinforcement based training methods.

I cover a wide range of training specialties, including:

  • Basic Obedience (sit, down, stay...)
  • Recall
  • Housetraining
  • Leash manners
  • Dancing
